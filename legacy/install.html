Installing on Mac

From download to first launch

Before you begin

Openbase can run commands and edit code in your local workspaces. Review changes, protect secrets, and keep backups. See the Terms of Service.

1

Find it in your Downloads

Your browser saves the disk image to the Downloads folder. Double-click it to open.

2

Open the disk image

macOS mounts the image and opens a window with the app and your Applications folder.

Drag it into Applications

Open it

Launch Openbase. If macOS asks, choose Open — or right-click the app and pick Open.

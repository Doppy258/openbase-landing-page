Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect account details, project metadata, usage analytics, and support communication history. We may also collect diagnostics from build and runtime events to improve reliability.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used to deliver the product, secure accounts, detect abuse, and improve platform performance. Aggregated usage patterns may be used for product planning and feature prioritization.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared with infrastructure, payment, and communications vendors acting on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.

4. Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Backup snapshots may persist for a limited period after deletion requests.

5. Your Choices

You can request account deletion, export project data, and opt out of non-essential product updates. Some data may still be retained where required by law or to prevent fraud and abuse.

6. Contact

For privacy requests, email privacy@openbase.cloud.
